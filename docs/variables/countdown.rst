$(countdown)
============

Resolves the time between now and the given date.

Usage:
    $(countdown ``<date>`` ``[time]``)

Example Command:
    **name**: !countdown

    **response**: The new year begins in $(countdown 1/1/2018 00:00:00)

    **output**::

        user:     !countdown
        botisimo: The new year begins in 30 days 12 hours 37 minutes
